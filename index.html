🩹 Basic First Aid Tips

Heart Attack: Call 108 immediately. Have patient sit and rest. Loosen clothing. Give aspirin if not allergic.

Choking: 5 back blows + 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver).

Bleeding: Firm pressure with clean cloth. Elevate the wound above heart level.

Burns: Cool with running water 10 minutes. No ice or oil. Cover loosely with clean cloth.
